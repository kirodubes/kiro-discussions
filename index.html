How it works

From a comment to the roadmap

Three steps. You post, the community votes, and the most-wanted ideas get built — into the Arch Linux Tweak Tool, the ISO, the themes, or wherever they fit.

step 1

Post it

Drop your feature request in Ideas, or answer a question in Polls. A sentence is plenty — describe what you'd like and why.

step 2

Vote it up

Upvote the ideas you want most. The board sorts by votes, so the top of the list is exactly what the community is asking for.

step 3

We build from the top

The roadmap follows demand. The most-wanted ideas get picked up and shipped — and you'll see them announced back here.

Guidelines

A good place to be

Every Linux distro is a good distro. This space is for making Kiro better — never for putting down what you run. Whatever you're on, you're welcome here.

A few house rules:
  • Be kind and respectful — to people and to other distros.
  • Search before you post, then upvote instead of duplicating.
  • This is for ideas & suggestions. Found a bug? Open it on the matching code repo, not here.
